About Us

Bharathi Vidyalaya Nursery and Primary School, located in Arni, offers quality English medium education from Pre-KG to 8th standard under the Matriculation syllabus. With a strong foundation built since 1988, we focus on discipline, individual attention and value-based learning. Our goal is not only academic excellence but also to nurture confidence, character and responsibility in every child. In a safe, caring and supportive environment, students are encouraged to explore, learn and grow into confident individuals.
