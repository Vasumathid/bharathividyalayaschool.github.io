About Us

A Legacy of Excellence in Arni

Bharathi Vidyalaya Nursery and Primary School, Arni, has been a trusted institution since 1988 — founded under the leadership of Thiru Gururajarao. In 2021, the school entered a new phase under GD Institutions, known for GD Vidyashram (Arcot) and GD IAS Academy.

Guided by Tmt. Gandhimathi Dulasidaran, M.A., M.Ed., the school provides a disciplined, structured environment where every student receives individual attention and holistic development.

  • Matriculation curriculum · Pre-KG to 8th Standard
  • LEAD integrated curriculum with smart learning methods
  • English medium instruction throughout
  • Daily tests, homework, and continuous assessment
  • Smart classes and activity-based learning
  • Strong Tamil language and reading skills program
  • Under GD Institutions — proven academic track record
