About Us

Bharathi Vidyalaya Nursery and Primary School, Arni, is a trusted institution with a legacy of excellence since 1988, founded under the leadership of Thiru Gururajarao. The school has consistently upheld strong academic standards, discipline, and value-based education, earning the confidence of the community over the years.

In 2021, the school entered a new phase under GD Institutions, known for GD Vidyashram, Arcot, and GD IAS Academy. With a proven track record in delivering strong academic and competitive results, the institution brings its expertise to Bharathi Vidyalaya with a clear focus on providing affordable, high-quality education for all.

Guided by Tmt. Gandhimathi Easwaran, M.A., M.Ed., the school ensures a disciplined and structured environment supported by dedicated women educators, where every student receives individual attention and holistic development.

Bharathi Vidyalaya continues to stand as a respected choice for parents in Arni, combining accessibility, academic strength, and a commitment to shaping confident and responsible individuals.

Principal’s Message
