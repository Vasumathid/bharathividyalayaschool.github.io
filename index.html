About Us

Bharathi Vidyalaya Nursery and Primary School, located in Arni, provides English medium education from Pre-KG to 8th standard under Matriculation syllabus. We focus on building a strong foundation for every child through discipline, individual attention and value-based education. Our aim is not only academic success, but also developing confidence, character and responsibility in students. In a safe and caring environment, children are encouraged to learn, grow and express themselves with confidence.
